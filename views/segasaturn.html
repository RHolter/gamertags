3D Lemmings is played by using four different, movable cameras to fly around and get an overview of the level. While some levels have fixed cameras, most of the time they can be freely moved at any time, although without the ability to tilt up or down. Another viewing option is the "virtual lemming" (VL) which allows the player to see through the eyes of a selected lemming.

Release Date(s): EU July 1995, Japan August 1996

Castlevania: Symphony of the Night begins during the ending of the previous game in the series, Castlevania: Rondo of Blood, where Richter Belmont confronts and defeats Count Dracula.Four years later, in 1796, Richter goes missing and Dracula's castle reappears.

Release Date(s): EU Novemeber 1997, Japan March 1997, North America October 1997
